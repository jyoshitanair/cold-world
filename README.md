### Description:
---

This is a puzzle based platformer that is similar to games  like fireboy and water girl. You are playing as two ferrets who have to unite to save their world from freezing over by beating the ultimate yeti. Each level allows you to have different buffs that make it easier. Work together using your different abilities and bodies to make it to the end!
